Why did we build this?

A while ago, my colleague Raji Gali pointed out that we rarely talk about testing or even encourage exercises around testing. We are one derivative away from testing in that we talk a lot with code/tools that will eventually be used to test. But we are not talking about testing directly.

This bias shows in many ways and affects our hiring, interviewing and sense of accomplishment within Qxf2. While there are multiple genuine reasons for this bias, it has been nagging me that we never directly focus on testing. This is our first attempt at a tool to solve this problem.
