What is this site?

This is a site meant for software testers. The site throws a random challenge that makes the tester think. We hope the challenges trigger conversations about testing with your colleagues and serve as a starting point for deeper discussions and fun explorations.

The challenges chosen are based on Qxf2's experience in hiring and training testers. We do not provide any answers. We just want the tester to think and discuss the challenges with their colleagues.
